Getting Started

Welcome to Kisan Mitra! Whether you're a farmer looking to sell your produce or a buyer searching for fresh goods, we're here to help you make the most of our platform.

What is Kisan Mitra?

Kisan Mitra is an online platform that connects Indian farmers directly with buyers, helping farmers get fair prices and buyers get fresh, quality produce without middlemen.

How do I sign up?

  • Go to the sign-up page.
  • Choose whether you are a Farmer or a Buyer.
  • Enter your basic details and create an account.
  • Verify your mobile number to start using the platform.

Finding and Purchasing Produce

How do I find fresh produce?

Use the search bar or browse through categories like Vegetables, Fruits, Grains, and Dairy to find the produce you need. You can view farmer profiles, product details, and connect with them directly.

How do I contact a farmer?

  • Go to the farmer's profile or product page.
  • Click on the Contact Farmer button to start a conversation.
  • Discuss product availability, quantity, pricing, and delivery options.
