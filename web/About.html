Our Story

At Kisan Mitra, we're revolutionizing agricultural marketing by directly connecting farmers with buyers. Our platform empowers rural communities, promotes sustainable farming, and ensures fair prices for quality produce.

Our Mission

Kisan Mitra was founded with a simple yet powerful vision: to create a sustainable ecosystem where farmers can thrive by connecting directly with buyers who value quality and authenticity.

We're dedicated to empowering rural communities by providing them with the digital tools and market access they need to grow their businesses and improve their livelihoods.

By eliminating intermediaries, we ensure farmers receive fair compensation for their hard work while buyers get access to fresh, high-quality produce straight from the source.

Our Journey

From a small initiative connecting local farmers to a nationwide platform, here's how we've grown.

The idea

Our team brainstormed solutions to help farmers connect directly with buyers, reducing middlemen and ensuring fair prices.

Planning & Research

We finalized the project scope, researched existing platforms, and designed the core features of Harvest Connect.

Development Begins

Started development of frontend and backend modules including user authentication, product listing, and buyer-seller interface.

Testing & Feedback

Conducted testing and refined features based on feedback from mentors and sample users.
